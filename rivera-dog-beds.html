The Maker

Hi, I'm Alejandro.

I'm a woodworker based out of New Orleans who became obsessed with one question: why do we spend so much love on our dogs, but still put them on the floor?

Every Rivera dog bed is built entirely by hand in my shop — solid wood frame, mortise-and-tenon joints, finished with a food-safe oil so it's safe for the pups. The slim mattress pad fits snug inside the frame like a crib.

I started selling on Facebook Marketplace and Instagram, and the response has been incredible. Every bed is made one at a time, measured to your dog. This is a startup built on sawdust and love — I hope you'll be part of it.

— Alejandro Rivera, Founder
